How it works

The public chain keeps the frame. The private draft stays off the board.

Auction setup becomes visible, bid commitments remain opaque, and users can read back the minted on-chain accounts from the same interface that collected the wallet signature.

Operating logic

  1. The seller creates an auction account with the visible terms the market needs.
  2. Bidders submit sealed bid receipts that link a private payload hash to the auction.
  3. The close page emits the final settlement request and records the explorer trail.

What stays hidden

  1. Raw bid drafts remain in the browser workspace instead of readable on-chain state.
  2. Losing bids do not need to become public content to prove that bidding happened.
  3. The operator still gets an auditable Solana footprint for every phase.

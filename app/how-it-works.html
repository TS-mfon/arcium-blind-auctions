How it works

Only the auction result needs to become public.

The seller publishes public terms. Bidders connect a wallet and submit encrypted bid inputs. Arcium computes the winner after close and the dapp reveals only the settlement output.

01

Create terms

Asset, reserve floor, supply, close time, and pricing mode are public and auditable.

02

Encrypt bids

Bid amount and quantity are handled as confidential inputs before computation.

03

Reveal result

The callback records the winner and clearing price after the auction closes.

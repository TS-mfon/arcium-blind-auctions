How it works

Sealed bids enter encrypted computation before settlement is public.

Arcium lets the app queue confidential computation from Solana. Bids are encrypted client-side, the MPC network computes the result, and the callback publishes only the verified settlement output.

1. Connect

The wallet signs Solana actions and gives the dapp a public key. The app never asks for private keys.

2. Encrypt

Bid values are converted into encrypted inputs for Arcium instead of being stored as public plaintext.

3. Verify

The deployed program and transactions are linked to Solana Explorer for independent verification.

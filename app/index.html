Arcium RTG Developer Submission

Blind Auctions with encrypted sealed bids

Bidders submit encrypted values. Arcium computes the winner and settlement price after close, revealing only the result needed for escrow settlement.

Modes

First-price, Vickrey, and fixed-size uniform auctions share the same private bid flow.

Private Inputs

Bid amount, valuation, and quantity stay encrypted during the live auction window.

Public Outputs

Winner, clearing price, claim amounts, refunds, and escrow settlement are published on-chain.

Demo Flow

  1. Create an auction and escrow the asset.
  2. Submit encrypted bids through the Arcium client.
  3. Close the auction and wait for the verified Arcium callback.
  4. Claim, refund, or withdraw according to the final settlement.
